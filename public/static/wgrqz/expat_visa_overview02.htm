　　　　 Yafei Realty 　　　　　　　　　　　　　Residential & Real Estate Services


In order to better meet the needs from the clients of our mother company “Beijing Yafei Business Visa Consulting Service Centre.”, Yafei Realty was founded in April, 2003 to provide professional support, advice and help for expatriating employees and their families on finding a suitable accommodation in Beijing. We will offer you our full attention and unlimited viewing opportunities within budget according to your specific requirements.

Yafei Realty is able to help you with:-

• Relocation services
• Residential search, viewing and lease negotiation
• Commercial office analysis, search, viewing and lease negotiation

Also as complement to our real estate services we also offer you a flexible and tailor-made orientation program to help you quickly learn your way around.

Yafei Realty Services

A deep knowledge of Beijing’s ever changing real estate market and a tremendous net of personal relationships with both developers and private landlords allow Yafei Realty to provide the most suitable property solutions for our corporate and individual clients. We provide guidance and expert advice during the home finding period, lease signing process and property hand-over/ hand-back stages as required. In every case, a “Home Search Analysis” is conducted to make sure that only the most suitable properties are selected for viewing. The “Analysis” also helps Yafei staff educate relocating individuals and families so that their expectations can be managed.

Yafei Realty Residential Services

We offer peace of mind when finding a new home in China. If you are an individual or a corporation it can be a difficult task for expatriates to search and find an appropriate home which caters to your needs and within your budget.

There are a number of services that can help you ease into China more effortlessly:-

• Searching, advising and providing details on potential homes
• Visiting homes (transport can be provided for ease of traveling)
• Lease negotiation on rental
• Lease signing and ongoing support
• Relocation services in China

We can also help you with purchasing the right furniture and décor for your new apartment to make you feel more at home in China.

Yafei Realty Commercial Office Services

Our services allow for professional search and negotiation of the right space to conduct your all important business in China. At the start of a business the right location will be invaluable to make sure you are close to your customers. We provide many corporate clients with many services to make the process that much easier. They include:-

• Company/business requirement analysis
• Searching, advising and providing details of potential premises
• Site visits (transport can be provided for ease of traveling)
• Lease negotiation on rental
• Lease signing and ongoing support
• Relocation services in China

Even if you require office furniture and equipment or help setting up your office, we help in the right setup and advise in Chinese office practices.
