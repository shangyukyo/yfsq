　　　　 Work Permit 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 Expatriate Affairs Services


Generally within 15 days of arriving in China and prior to beginning employment, the foreign employee should apply to the Chinese Labor Bureau for a work permit. After obtaining the work permit, the employee can apply for his/her residence permit at the local Public Security Bureau.

Documents & information required

• Original Employment license
• Original Medical Examination Record
• Original Passport and copies of pages with photo and with valid Z-Visa
• Copy of business license of Chinese employer
• Copy of employment contract
• Application forms filled out and stamped by the employer

Time Required C 5 working days
